    • Cooking!

    Cooking has been a passion of mine since I was 7 years old. While other kids were watching cartoons
    I was watching the Frugal Gourmet, Lydia, and Jack Pepin on the Public Broadcasting Channel. The first thing
    I ever cooked was a potato, brocolli, and cheddar soup that I saw the frugal gourmet make. I remember clearly how
    the epiphany struck me that I had all of the ingredients he was using inside of my refrigerator and therefore, I too could
